Our Story

Rooted in the
Hill Country

Redbud Kitchen was born from a simple belief: the best meals come from honest ingredients, good company, and a deep respect for the land they come from.

Nestled in the heart of Wimberley, we partner with family farms across the Texas Hill Country to bring you a menu that changes with the seasons. What's on your plate today was likely growing just down the road yesterday.

We cook with wood-fired techniques passed down through generations — slow, intentional, and built around the kind of flavors that linger long after the last bite. Every table at Redbud is an invitation to slow down and savor.

Clara & James Whitfield
Founders & Executive Chefs
